Description

Professor Theo has been kidnapped by the imperial forces of the evil empire. Now his robotic-personal assistant, Marina Liteyears, must save the day! Marina will get some help from the troops who have remained loyal to King Aster. With their help, she'll Grab, Shake and Throw her way to the Professor!

Besides rescuing the Professor, Marina will also join in the effort to rebel against that Nasty, Horrible and evil empire. So, Grab a controller and help our Super Heroine Shake up the evil empire!
