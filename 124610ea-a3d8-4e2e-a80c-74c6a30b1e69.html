Description

Adventure so real, you'll feel the sweat!

The legendary Pitfall! is back! Twelve years later, the classic returns with ground-breaking gameplay that'll rattle your nerves and leave you in a cold sweat...

Join Harry Jr., son of Pitfall Harry, as he tracks an evil Mayan warrior spirit, to rescue his kidnapped father... Run, swing, skate and bungee through deep dark rainforests, raging waterfalls and Mayan templ ruins that reek of lethal enemies and deadly traps...until you face the master of evil - Zakelua.

Gripping gameplay! Experience the danger of exploration in level after nerve-wracking level of secret regions and hidden escape routes!

Magical, possessed and relentless enemies! Pit your skills against sinister Mayan spirits, hungry crocodiles, wicked skeletons, fiery pendulums, bottomless pits and more.

Feature film-quality animation! Award-winning Kroyer Films jolts Harry Jr. to life with split-second reflexes and animated reations that often take you by surprise...

Pulse-pounding jungle soundtrack! Featuring authentic sounds recorded deep in the jungles of Central America by Academy Award-winning Soundelux Media Labs.
