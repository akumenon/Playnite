Description

Whel aliens from planet FM plot to invade Earth, alien Omega-Xis escapes to warn a courageous boy, Geo Stelar. Together, they must face the aliens on the electromagnetic system known as the Wave Road, and combine to become the ultimate champion for Earth; Mega Man!

Star Force DX completely modernises Pegasus, Leo, and Dragon to include many new features, changes, and tweaks that bring the game up to speed with its sequels. It adds depth and replayability, unifies all 3 versions of the game, reduces reliance on multi-player, and much more.
