Description

You are the last surviving vampire slayer facing the hordes of Dracula himself.

In the dark abyss, on the floating remains of his ruined castle, you are all that stands between our world, and theirs...

Your arsenal consists of a limited number of tools: your shotgun, your grappling hook, and your trusty legs. Use these to the best of your ability to outsmart the enemy and send them all to hell!
